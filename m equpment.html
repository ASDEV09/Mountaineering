Equipment

For a successful day out in the mountains bringing the right equipment is essential. The Icelandic weather can change very rapidly and experiencing all four seasons in one day is possible.

Climbing Gear

  • Climbing Harness (Climbing belt)
  • Climbing Helmet (Helmet)
  • Climbing Shoes (Climbing shoes)
  • Crampons (Crampons)
  • Ice Axe (Ice axe)
  • Carabiners (Locking and non-locking carabiners)
  • Quickdraws (Quickdraws)
  • Slings (Sling)
  • Prusik Cords (Prusik cords)
  • Belay Device (Belay device)

Clothing

  • Waterproof and Windproof Hardshell Jacket (Waterproof and windproof jacket)
  • Waterproof and Windproof Hardshell Pants (Waterproof and windproof pants)
  • Insulated Down or Synthetic Jacket (Insulated jacket)
  • Moisture-wicking Base Layers (Top and bottom layers)
  • Insulating Layers (Fleece or down layers)
  • Climbing Gloves (Waterproof and insulated gloves)
  • Mountaineering Socks (Socks)
  • Gaiters (To keep snow out of your boots)

Safety Equipment

  • Avalanche Safety Gear (Transceiver, probe, and shovel) (Avalanche safety equipment)
  • First Aid Kit (First aid kit)
  • Emergency Bivy or Shelter (Emergency shelter)
  • Headlamp with Extra Batteries (Headlamp with spare batteries)
  • Map and Compass (Map and compass)
  • GPS Device (Optional) (GPS device - as per requirement)
  • Whistle (Whistle)
  • Sunglasses with UV Protection (Sunglasses with UV protection)
  • Sunscreen and Lip Balm with High SPF (Sunscreen and lip balm with high SPF)
